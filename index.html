Who i am

In short, I'm just a small guy looking to participate in something big and learn as much as i can

I am a usual person in this world, who likes to participate in team projects and team activities.

Since i can remember i always have been in teams in order to get biggeer achivments than an individual can get in his own way. I could say that I am a person who needs people around me to get the best possible results.

I am a spanish programmer studying in Sheffield Hallam University, England. Before that I developed Old Town Stories(Oculus Rift, PC, Steam). During my studies I have gained experience developing for PC and mobile across multiple disciplines, though I feel most comfortable in core programming and physics related gameplay programming. I always want to learn from the people around me and grow as a coder and person to be able to be a better version of myself every day. That's why I always try to surround myself with the best people possible, work as well as possible and grow, especially grow. My passion for physics is real, If I had not been a programmer I would have studied physics, it is something that has always attracted me and always attracted me
